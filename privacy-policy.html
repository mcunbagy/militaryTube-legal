Privacy Policy

MilitaryTube  ·  Last updated: May 19, 2026

1. Introduction

MilitaryTube ("we," "our," or "the app") is a military news and defense intelligence application. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use our app, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using MilitaryTube, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information.

Your watchlist (countries and equipment you save) is stored anonymously on our servers, linked only to a randomly generated device identifier — never to your identity.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device Advertising Identifier (IDFA / Advertising ID)
With your permission (see Section 5 — Advertising), we access your device's advertising identifier to serve personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. This identifier is a resettable, non-permanent ID assigned by your operating system. You can reset or limit it at any time in your device settings.

IP Address
Collected automatically by our cloud infrastructure (Firebase/Google) and by advertising networks when the app makes network requests. Used for basic service operation and regional ad targeting.

App Usage Data
Firebase collects anonymized usage data (e.g., which screens are visited, crash reports) to help us maintain and improve the app. This data is not linked to your identity.

Purchase Information
If you purchase a premium subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple. We receive only your subscription status (active/expired) through RevenueCat. We never see or store your payment details.

Push Notification Token
If you enable watchlist notifications, a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token is generated and stored on our servers. This token is used solely to deliver notifications about content related to your watchlist. It is not shared with third parties.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email, phone number, or any account credentials
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Health or biometric data
  • Financial or payment information

5. Advertising and App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

MilitaryTube displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

On iOS 14.5 and later, we request permission to use your device's advertising identifier under Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. A system prompt will appear asking:

"MilitaryTube would like permission to track you across apps and websites owned by other companies."
  • If you allow tracking: you will see personalized ads relevant to your interests.
  • If you decline tracking: you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized. Your privacy is fully preserved.

You can change this preference at any time in:
iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → MilitaryTube

Google also requires separate consent for EU/EEA users under GDPR. If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you will see an additional consent screen on first launch. You may decline without losing access to the app.

7. Data Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS. Firestore (cloud database) access is protected by Firebase Security Rules — your watchlist data can only be accessed by your own anonymous device session. We do not have access to your payment information at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

MilitaryTube contains military and geopolitical content intended for general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the app, please contact us and we will delete any associated data.

9. Your Rights

European Union / EEA (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU or EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your anonymous data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw advertising consent at any time (see Section 5)
  • Complaint: Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

California (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected and how it is used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

All Users

  • Delete your data in-app: Settings → Account → Delete My Data
  • Reset advertising ID: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Disable personalized ads: iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising
  • Request data deletion by email: Contact us at the email below

10. Account Deletion & Data Removal

MilitaryTube automatically creates an anonymous account when you first open the app. No email address or personal identity is required or collected. This account stores your watchlist, notification preferences, timezone, and language setting.

You have the right to delete all data associated with your account at any time.

How to delete your data

  1. Open the app and go to Settings
  2. Scroll to the Account section
  3. Tap Delete My Data
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog

This action permanently deletes:

  • Your watchlist (saved countries and equipment)
  • Your push notification token
  • Your language and timezone preferences
  • Your anonymous Firebase account record

What is not affected: Active subscriptions are managed exclusively by Apple through the App Store. Deleting your data does not cancel any active subscription. To cancel a subscription, go to iOS Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions. RevenueCat purchase receipts associated with your account will be anonymized upon deletion.

Data deletion is permanent and irreversible.
